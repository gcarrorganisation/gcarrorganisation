BIO

I'm a soon-to-graduate film student studying at University of Bristol. I have studied documentary making, animation, film history, television formats, British horror and contemporary Hollywood. I have jumped at every opportunity university has offered me, working for the media societies such as UBTV, Studiospace and InHouse Media. I was also involved in the establishment of a new, inclusive arts society, BUCAS, this year following the success of an event in June. I am keen to find a career following my graduation in June 2018.

I have particular interests in culture, gender representation, classical civilisations, foreign-language film, art-house film and ancient mythology.
